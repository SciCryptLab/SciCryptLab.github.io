What it is

The check parses the References/Bibliography section out of the uploaded main article, detects each entry's DOI, and validates it against the Crossref API — politely rate-limited so it stays a good citizen of the service.

What it can do

  • Five-signal cross-check. Author (30), title (30), year (20), journal (10), volume (10) — compared against the Crossref record.
  • Clear per-reference status. matched (≥70), partial (40–69), mismatched (<40), invalid (lookup failed), or no_doi.
  • Fabrications surface fast. A DOI that doesn't resolve is flagged outright.
  • Evidence on tap. Every non-matching reference shows its signal breakdown for editor review.
