SciVerify · feature

Fluent isn't the same as written by a person.

SciVerify applies 15 heuristic indicators across every IMRaD section — from pasted chatbot prefixes and tortured phrasing to hallucinated citations and statistical cadence anomalies — and highlights the exact words that triggered each flag.

What it is

A transparent, heuristics-based screen — not a black-box classifier. Fifteen indicators are organized into severity tiers and applied per section, so a fluent discussion section that's drifting from the rest of the paper doesn't slip through. An optional local-LLM verdict adds a holistic second opinion.

What it can do

  • 15 indicators, five tiers. From critical signals like chatbot leaks and tortured paraphrase to subtler ones like cadence, topical drift and internal inconsistency.
  • Per-section scoring. Introduction, methods, results, discussion and conclusion are scored separately.
  • Trigger words highlighted. Click a flag and see exactly what set it off.
  • Banded verdicts. Sections fall into likely-AI, partial, or human bands.
  • Cached & re-runnable. Results invalidate automatically when the main article is re-uploaded.
