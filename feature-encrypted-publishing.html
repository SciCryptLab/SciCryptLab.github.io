What it is

SciTrace's publish pipeline encrypts a dataflow with AES-256-GCM and wraps the key with RSA-OAEP, pins the encrypted payload to IPFS via Kubo, and anchors a fingerprint on-chain through the MetadataStorage smart contract. Promotions between tiers go through an admin approval queue, so nothing is published by accident.

What it can do

  • Four storage tiers. local → ipfs → anchored → archive, each with its own guarantees.
  • Encryption by default. AES-256-GCM payloads with RSA-OAEP key wrapping; recipients decrypt with an at-rest key or a hardware token.
  • Tamper-evident. An on-chain anchor lets anyone verify the published bytes haven't changed.
  • Cross-tier verification. One click confirms a dataflow exists consistently across disk, IPFS and chain.
  • Approval-gated. Tier promotions and publish requests pass through an admin queue.
