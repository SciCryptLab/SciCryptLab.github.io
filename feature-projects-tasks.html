What it is

Projects give your dataflows a home: a name, a description, a research type, and a list of collaborators. Tasks track the work to be done against that project, each carrying a priority (low, medium, urgent) and a status (pending, ongoing, done).

What it can do

  • Group dataflows. Keep related datasets under one project with shared context.
  • Invite collaborators. Attach the people working on the project.
  • Track tasks. Capture what's next with a priority and a status, without leaving SciTrace.
  • Stay in flow. The plan and the data live in the same place — no separate tracker to keep in sync.
