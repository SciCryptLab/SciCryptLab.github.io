What it is

The check extracts the author block from the top of the manuscript — handling superscript markers, bracketed labels, plain digits and unicode affiliation symbols — and verifies each author against OpenAlex on three independent signals.

The three signals

  • Existence. An OpenAlex record with at least one indexed work, or a syntactically valid ORCID in the manuscript.
  • Linkage. The author is tied to an ORCID and/or affiliation for identity verification.
  • Field-match. The author's concept tags overlap the manuscript's topics (Jaccard ≥ 0.2).

Each author lands in a clear pass / partial / fail bucket.
